# SmartDigitalMinds

SmartDigitalMinds builds and runs practical AI agents for business workflows where speed, follow-up, reporting, and consistency create measurable value.

## Services

- Agent audits: Review a business workflow, current tools, weekly volume, handoff points, delays, and value opportunities before recommending an AI agent implementation.
- Lead intake agents: Capture website forms, missed calls, quote requests, new inquiries, and handoffs before leads go cold.
- Follow-up agents: Send timely next-step reminders, review requests, status updates, and lead follow-up messages based on clear business rules.
- Back-office agents: Reduce repeated administrative work across scheduling, data cleanup, summaries, routing, approvals, and internal requests.
- Reporting agents: Turn daily activity into weekly numbers, alerts, management views, and practical recommendations.
- Operations automation: Map repetitive workflows, connect data and tools, define escalation paths, and monitor outcomes after launch.
- WhatsApp integration: Support inbound, permission-first WhatsApp audit requests and prepare agent-assisted replies, handoffs, templates, and reporting when the business channel is approved.
- AI governance and readiness scoping: Help identify workflow, tool, data, volume, pain-point, market, and timeline requirements for structured AI implementations, including ISO 42001-oriented discovery when relevant.

## Operating Model

SmartDigitalMinds starts with the business case. A useful agent should reduce operating drag, recover revenue, improve response time, or make management reporting clearer.

The delivery model is:

1. Map the workflow, data, decision points, and owner.
2. Build the agent behavior, integrations, messages, and reporting.
3. Run the system with monitoring, quality checks, and escalation paths.
4. Improve the agent based on measured value and real usage.
